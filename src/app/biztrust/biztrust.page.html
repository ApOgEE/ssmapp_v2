INTRODUCTION
SSM BizTrust is a business starter kit that serves as digital identification of business entities registered with the Companies Commission of Malaysia (SSM).
OBJECTIVE
The objective of SSM BizTrust business starter kit is to strengthen the compliance with business entities through the provisions of the Acts administered by SSM with validation through SSM BizTrust QR code.
FUNCTION
This method of reviewing merchant information via SSM BizTrust QR code will display infomratin such as business name, business registration number, status and business URL address (if any)
